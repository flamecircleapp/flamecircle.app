Location & Map Data

FlameCircle includes an interactive global map displaying fire spinning meet-ups, jams, events, and community locations worldwide.

How Location Data Works

  • Map pins: Event and location data shown on the map is either submitted by community members or collected from publicly available sources. No individual tracking occurs.
  • Your device location: If you grant location permission, we use your approximate location to centre the map and suggest nearby events. This is processed on-device and is not stored on our servers without your explicit permission.
  • Weather data: Weather forecasts for event locations are fetched from a weather API (such as Open-Meteo, a free and privacy-respecting service). Only the coordinates of the event you are viewing are sent — never your personal device location.
  • Publicly submitted events: If you submit an event with a location, that location becomes part of the public map data visible to all users.
You can use the FlameCircle map without granting location access. Location permission is optional and can be revoked at any time in your device settings.

Automated Data Collection (Map)

FlameCircle periodically collects publicly available information about fire spinning events from the open internet to keep the map up to date. This process:

  • Only collects information that is publicly posted online (e.g. event listings, community announcements)
  • Does not collect any personal data beyond publicly listed event details (organiser name, date, location, description)
  • Complies with the terms of service of the sources used
  • Does not store information about private individuals without their consent

Children's Privacy

FlameCircle is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. Fire spinning and flow arts involve real safety risks, and our platform discusses fire performance, fuel handling, and live performance environments.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at privacy@flamecircle.app and we will delete that data promptly.

For users aged 13–17, parental or guardian consent is required to create an account.
